To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer support team with your order number and details about the issue. For more information, please refer to our "Returns and Exchanges" page on our website..

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer support team with your order number, details about the issue, and any relevant photos. We will work with you to resolve the issue by offering a replacement, refund, or exchange, depending on the specific situation.

Specific shipping options and delivery times will be provided during the checkout process.
